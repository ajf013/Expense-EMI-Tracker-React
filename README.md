## About The App

The **Expense & EMI Tracker** is a modern, responsive web application designed to help you manage your monthly finances with ease. Built with React and Vite, it offers a clean and intuitive interface to track your income, expenses, and monthly loan payments (EMIs).

### Key Features

*   **Financial Overview**: Instantly view your total balance, total expenses, and monthly EMI obligations at a glance.
*   **EMI Management**: 
    *   Add and track monthly EMIs with details like loan name, amount, total tenure, and start date.
    *   **Auto-Calculated Progress**: Visual indicators show how many months you've paid and how many are remaining.
    *   **Paid Status Toggle**: innovative "Paid" toggle to mark EMIs as paid for the current month.
*   **Quick Expense Entry**: Easily add daily expenses by category (Food, Travel, Bills, etc.).
*   **Smart Monthly Reset**: The app automatically detects a new month and resets your expense list and EMI paid statuses while preserving your core salary and EMI settings.
*   **Customizable Aesthetics**: Toggle between a crisp Light Mode and a sleek, vibrant Dark Mode.
*   **Shareable Live View**: Generate a unique, read-only link to share your current financial snapshot with others without giving them edit access.
*   **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for seamless use on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
